WHAT SETS EAIT APART?

EAIT Value Propostion
AI is being scaled across industries at an enterprise level. By collecting and learning from data, AI systems can spot trends and provide insights to help improve agility, accuracy, efficiency and productivity. Costs are cut, whilst speed and consistency are improved, particularly when the collaborative interface between humans and AI is nurtured and optimized.

Through EAIT’s partnered AI ecosystem, we provide a platform from which to develop big ideas and answer universal questions that rest at the very heart of AI evolution – social, economic, environmental, technological and ethical. Through thought leadership, education and a meeting of minds at our global AI conference, we aspire to harness the profound benefits AI solutions can make to promote enterprise and address the global challenges governments, businesses and communities face in the digital age.

Innovate, Educate
We nurture innovation in AI science and in the potential that AI offers industries, economies and societies. We invest in the education and training needed to harness the benefits of AI and to maintain development momentum, through academic platforms and the reskilling of staff across industry.

Automate, Augment, integrate
We create technological advantage and deliver business improvement. We directly support local industry automation and augmentation through AI integration, turning AI enabled benefit into tangible business advantage.
