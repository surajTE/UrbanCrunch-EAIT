AI THOUGHT LEADERSHIP

Strategic knowledge transfer, open communication and expert engagement are core to EAIT’s goals of delivering impactful business and social solutions. We leverage the extensive expertise inherent across our partnered ecosystem to engage and promote thought leadership across multiple channels, not least our commitment to world leading AI Conferences.

AI EDUCATION TRAINING & QUALIFICATION

Industry has a vital role in upskilling workforces for the AI age. Likewise, businesses have both commercial and moral incentives to do so. These obligations become even more vital if the speed of AI-driven change means our existing education and training bodies can’t keep pace. Thus, EAIT promotes AI education growth and skills development training at graduate and post-graduate level. We nurture the AI leaders of tomorrow and advocate the integration of AI with human talent. We support businesses directly in the training of staff (upskilling and reskilling) in order to meet the ever-evolving pace and complexity of the digital era.

AI RESEARCH, DEVELOPMENT & SUPPORT

At the heart of EAIT is our research incubation capacity, driven in conjunction with our prestigious partnership with Harrisburg University (USA) and collaboration with Khalifa University (UAE). We nurture a culture of ideas, innovation, and progress by leveraging our access to rich data sources and utilizing our unique location in the technologically advanced Abu Dhabi Global Market (ADGM). We support AI entrepreneurs and start-ups to incubate and introduce AI enabled disruption to the markets.

AI TRANSFORMATION 
AUTOMATION, AUGMENTATION, 
INNOVATION

As the potential of AI grows every day, so does corporate pressure to use it. But in the rush to implement AI powered solutions without a larger vision and strategy in place, initiatives often remain siloed with limited ROI potential. We facilitate unleashing the full power of AI by transforming the entire business - not just in small pockets, but at scale. We will build a strong AI strategy that empowers you to invest in the right systems, build responsible practices, and prepare your business and people for tomorrow. We apply analytics and automation to identify and articulate your unique AI and IoT strategy and help you scale the impact systemically across the organization. We will help you to design, select and implement the right AI driven solution to automate, augment and integrate, enabling the productivity, efficiency and differentiation necessary to position for sustainable growth in the digital economy.

AI PILOT TEST DESIGN & EXECUTION

Where applicable, we will undertake pilots for ‘fast-fail’ Quality Assurance and Quality Control (QA/QC) testing under controlled conditions to help develop and authenticate your wider enterprise strategic AI design.

AI' SUPERTEAM’ DEVELOPMENT

AI is a constellation of technologies that allow machines to sense, comprehend, act and learn in order to extend our capabilities. Our customers see the greatest gains when humans and machines work together to complement each other's strengths. AI will work next to humans in their organizations, as a co-worker, innovator and trusted advisor and EAIT will assist in framing and implementing the skills, processes and technologies needed to create this integration. We will foster the harmony necessary to enable and leverage this ‘AI Superteam’ construct where the whole is greater than the sum of its parts.

CYBER SERVICES & SECURITY

In conjunction with our extensive partners, we will ‘red team’ your digital security measures and protocols to identify and resolve cyber vulnerabilities.
